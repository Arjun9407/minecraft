ABOUT CRAFT

YOUR MINECRAFT HUB SINCE 2024

Craft was built by a small team of dedicated Minecraft players who got tired of scattered, outdated seed lists and command references. We wanted one place where every seed is tested, every command is verified, and every tip actually works.

Our team tests every seed on the latest Java and Bedrock versions. We document exact coordinates, note version-specific differences, and verify every build tip in creative mode before publishing.

For survival tips, we run speed tests and comparison builds to prove which strategies actually save time. No hearsay, no outdated advice from 2019. Every tip is current for 2026 gameplay.

OUR MISSION

Make the technical and creative sides of Minecraft accessible to everyone, from day-one players to veteran redstone engineers and master builders.
